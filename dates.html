2022 Ideal Village – WGF Conference Agenda

Day 1 Evening Reception and Special Keynote – Dr Sudhir Jain, Vice Chancellor, BHU
Days 2 and 3 below:

Four Plenary sessions of all participants (90 minutes) plus lunch and dinner keynotes.

  • Global Rural Development Efforts: AARDO, ENRD, US, Canada, Israel: Initiatives & BP examples
  • National outlook: NITI Aayog – Challenges/Needs, Initiatives, Collaboration, Lessons
  • State level: select from UP/Gujarat/AP/T Nadu/Maharashtra/Odisha/MP/Kerala - Empowerment of Rural communities. (Fireside chats)
  • Framework for Academic Collaborative R&D (IITs/Stanford/Amrita/IIRD/IRMA/NIRD-PR & other Universities)

Multiple parallel sessions (90-120 minutes) on Technology Applications / Showcase NGO Projects of the Best Practice Winners.

Four Half days sessions, with a Keynote speaker & 3 Best Practice winners (1st 5 tracks)

  • Agriculture & Farming: agritech, organic farming, high yields, low water usage, innovative solutions
  • Healthcare & Nutrition: Telemedicine, Access/Digital clinics, Mobile clinics, Public health, rural hospitals
  • Education & Skilling: rural campuses, vocational, on-line / digital, community, girls, skills development
  • Water and sanitation, waste management: rainwater harvesting, conservation, low cost filtration, clean drinking water, STP….
  • Energy and Environment: clean energy (solar, wind, hydro…), energy storage, microgrids, Climate change management
  • Livelihoods & Sustainability: Agriculture, Rural SMEs, Social entrepreneurship, Women empowerment
  • Adarsh Grama / Ideal Village Success Stories – best showcase

WORKSHOP Format (3x90min sessions) leading to recommendations & action plans
  • Empowerment & Social Inclusion
  • Micro-financing and credit facilities
  • Rural Administration, Policy framework, Legal structures and Governance

EXHIBITION & POSTER SESSION
For different stakeholders (Payment for stalls)
Mobile exhibition that can travel to various States.

ivwgf2022imbhu.in

WGF Conference will be held in India in view of the Indian Government’s major push in rural development. This is to initiate a global movement in Integrated Rural Development.

Given the high quality and diversity of topics, speakers, organizations and participants over the past six years, the theme for 2022 Ideal Village Conference will be “Rural Transformation for Sustainable Growth” to capture the lessons learnt and best practices from successful implementations and thereby define the future of global rural development.

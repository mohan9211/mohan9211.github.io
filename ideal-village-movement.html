Ideal Village Movement

Vision

Enable the essential needs in Water, Health, Education, Energy and Livelihoods, in a Holistic and Sustainable manner, using cost-effective technology and stakeholder-collaboration to create an enduring rural economy for 20% of India’s rural population by 2030.

Mission & Methodology

Identify best practices from NGOs/Foundations/Academia/SMART Village methodology for scaling across India to create Ideal Villages.​Receive buy-in from Central Govt. and State Govt. departments and relevant agencies to enable scaling to achieve the impact goals.​Integrate CSR programs and Companies that align with each Ideal Village Track (vertical) to enable expanded pilots and district-level scaling.​Maintain a central Ideal Village Project Office and satellite centers for all States involved in the Ideal Village scaling programs.​Recruit Ideal Village champions at all levels who will ensure integrity, strict adherence, and consistent progress of the rural development mission. ​Institutes of Eminence (IITs, BHU, etc) mentor 5-10 regional colleges to disseminate  Ideal Village knowhow and research output for scaling.​

Metrics of Success​

  • Each IV Track will be monitored by a Steering Committee from a set of diverse private-public institutions. 

  • Baseline study of all villages/districts involved in the Mission to assess current status and needs.​

  • Pilot and scaling solutions for each Ideal Village Track will depend on local needs & status.​

  • Solutions will be selected from approved set of Best Practices in each Ideal Village Track.​

  • Implementation plans defined after taking into consideration the baseline data at village, cluster, district levels.​

  • Progress towards Ideal Village goals will be measured on half-yearly basis for corrective actions. ​

  • Success metrics to be defined on a statistically valid basis by a team of experts in the field.​

  • Launch Global (International) Institutes for Rural Development at Stanford and IIT-BHU as models.​

  • Highlight success stories, potential best practices in each Track for future scaling​

  • Mobilize NGOs, Foundations, Corporates, Govt. agencies to be part of the IVM​

  • 2022 Conference to serve as kickoff for the IVM launch and awareness campaign​

  • Define core committees for each Ideal Village Track to help launch the IVM​

  • Create impetus and structure for Fundraising for the Ideal Village Movement ​

India as the global model for Rural Development​

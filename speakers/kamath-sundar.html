Kamath, Dr. Sundar

Dr. Sundar Kamath,
Senior VP / CTO, Sanmina Corporation, CA.

Dr. Sundar Kamath is Senior VP and CTO at Sanmina Corporation, an integrated design and manufacturing solutions provider with headquarters in Silicon Valley and operations in 20 countries, including a state of the art electronics manufacturing campus on a 100 acre site in Oragadam (Kancheepuram Dist), Tamil Nadu.

Prior to Sanmina, Dr. Kamath held technology development, project and product management positions at IBM Microelectronics, East Fishkill, NY, and MicroModule Systems Inc., Cupertino, CA, covering a range of semiconductor and microelectronics packaging technologies and products. He is a co-inventor on a dozen US patents, and has contributed to numerous technical publications and conference presentations at various industry forums including IEEE, ASME, SMTA, IMAPS, IPC, MRS, etc., and co-authored 2 chapters for a University level text on Microsystems Packaging. He has been an advisor to startups in Silicon Valley and Bangalore (India), and served on academic advisory boards. He is a past Board member of the International Electronics Manufacturing Initiative (iNEMI) and charter member of TIE (The Indus Entrepreneurs) Silicon Valley.

He holds Bachelor’s, Master’s and Ph.D. degrees in engineering from the University of Madras (India), Cranﬁeld Institute of Technology (England), and the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign respectively.

In 2015, he led the Science for Society project to develop the ﬁrst special-purpose vehicle that had a built-in medical clinic and internet connected classroom, all powered by solar panels and storage batteries, to improve access to these essential services for rural communities in India.
He has co-chaired the Ideal Village conference held at Stanford since 2016.

idealvillageconference.com
7th Ideal Village Conference will be held in India in view of the Indian Government’s major push in rural development. This is to initiate a global movement in Integrated Rural Development.

Given the high quality and diversity of topics, speakers, organizations and participants over the past six years, the theme for 2022 Ideal Village Conference will be “Rural Transformation for Sustainable Growth” to capture the lessons learnt and best practices from successful implementations and thereby define the future of global rural development.
